FAQ

I can't sign in.

Check your phone number and try again. Make sure SMS is available on your device.

Location isn't working.

Go to iOS Settings -> Credi -> Location -> "While Using the App."

I want to delete my account.

Email us at crediresolution@gmail.com from the phone number used to sign in.
